Hey Beach Players!

This is a special e-mail to all that have ever registered for our beach hatter.

Firstly, to everyone that has registered in all of our hatters, we want you to take a moment to help learn the story of Corinne & Rocco and their tragic accident. They are lovely people that volunteer their time every day to grow the sport of ultimate in the Tampa Bay area that on New Year's eve suffered an intense blow from a drunk uninsured driver, leaving them in all worlds of emotional, financial, and physical pain.

Please take a moment to hear their story, learn about it, and contribute or share their cause. People like Rocco & Corrine help make the game we enjoy possible, by building and supporting the communities we now are accustomed to. To learn more visit:

  • https://www.gofundme.com/help-corinne-and-rocco-recover

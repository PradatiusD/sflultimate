South Florida, Fall League is Back!

We are very excited to announce that ultimate is back at Amelia Earhart Park for South Florida Ultimate fall league!

Regular season games will be Wednesday evenings at 8pm, starting October 6th.

We're planning on 8 weeks of play on Wednesdays, skipping the Wednesday of Thanksgiving week and ending on Wednesday, December 1st. Finals are planned for December 12th, and will be at Amelia Earhart Park.

Registration ends Tuesday, October 5th at midnight EST and the costs are:

  • $30 for students
  • $50 for everyone else

Player fees will be put toward fields, lights, insurance, and jerseys for the league.

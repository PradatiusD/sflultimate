Welcome everyone!

With a new fall comes a brand new, revolutionary way to play Ultimate Frisbee.

Florida ranks #2 in beach coastline with 1,350 miles of pristine, gorgeous, sunny, beaches (trivia: Alaska is #1 but surprisingly no one likes playing beach frisbee there).

Yet we haven't taken advantage of this wondrous gift.

For this reason, we've decided for this fall and winter to offer our first organized Beach Ultimate League.

>We'll games on Saturday mornings at 10am near the beach space by Park & Ocean staring November, 4th and played until December 9th. Our finals would be on December 16th.

Following this league we would transition to a Winter Beach League managed and run by the wonderful Fiasco ladies (more information to follow).

The cost would be significantly reduced, most likely $10, making it one of our cheapest leagues ever.

The games would be 4v4 with either a 3-1 or 2-2 gender ratio. We'll be looking to find a competitive & fun level of play.

We'll be hosting the draft on October 21st, and we're looking for captains.

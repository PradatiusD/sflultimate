A quick reminder before tomorrow's games.

While jerseys are being ordered, please bring a white jersey/shirt and a colored jersey (ideally matching the color of team that you are on). This helps to make it easy to recognize which player is on what team.

Also please note that Ives Estates park is an Astro Turf field (artificial grass). While it's soft to land on, drains well when it rains, and remains in good state year-round, it tends to leave rug-burn like marks when a player slides, so just adjust your attire accordingly.
